Setup ChromeDriver

Back to Home

1 Update Google Chrome

To ensure everything works smoothly, simply update your browser to the latest version.

1. Open Chrome.

2. Go to Settings > About Chrome.

3. Wait for it to update to the latest version.

2 Download ChromeDriver

Get the latest "Stable" driver from the official dashboard.

Go to Download Page

Instructions:

  • Look for the green Stable section.
  • Find the chromedriver row.
  • Copy/Paste the URL for win64 (or your OS) into your browser to download.
  • Unzip the file and select chromedriver.exe in this app.
